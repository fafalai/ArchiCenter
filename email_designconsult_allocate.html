Before you meet your architect

We’ve given your architect an overview of your discussion with us to help start the conversation when you meet. Your architect will be in touch with you over the next few days to arrange a suitable time to meet. If you haven’t heard from your architect within three days of this booking confirmation please call us.

Before you meet it’s a good idea to start pulling together some notes on the project. Think about the current issues or limitations of your home/site and create a wish list of the things you’d like to include or improve in your new home. Don’t forget to have a realistic indication of your timing and budget. Are you aware of any limitations or restrictions that apply to your property such as heritage, landscape or planning overlays or a covenant? If you have any drawings of your property make sure you have a copy available for your conversation with your architect. A Certificate of Title and associated site plan described thereby will be most helpful.

There are a lot of things to consider and this meeting is a great time to get a better understanding of the journey you are about to embark on. You’ll find helpful hints on our website to help you prepare for your meeting.
