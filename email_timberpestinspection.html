The Report

The Report will have noted a number of items that require repair or maintenance. If you are considering purchasing the property, we trust the report may assist you in making allowance in your budget for items that require expenditure or; negotiating a better purchase price.

  • Unrestricted access to all parts of a property is in practice seldom available at the time of assessment but the report is prepared with care and diligence within the limits of available access.

Balconies and Decks

We recommend annual assessments of external timber/concrete decks and balconies be undertaken to verify their soundness. If in doubt, advice from a structural engineer or building surveyor is essential.
